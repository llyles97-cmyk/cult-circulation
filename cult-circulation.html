About the Publication

Culture Intelligence,
Documented.

Cult Circulation is an independent culture intelligence publication tracking how culture moves through music, fandom, nightlife, internet communities, fashion, technology, and the systems that govern influence. We report on what's shifting before the mainstream catches it — and we explain why.

Editorial Mission

What Is Cult Circulation?

Cult Circulation was founded on a single conviction: that culture moves in ways most publications aren't equipped to follow. The typical culture beat chases what's already visible. We document what's moving underneath — the signals that precede the trend, the communities that precede the mainstream, the moments that precede the narrative.

We combine editorial storytelling with audience intelligence and first-person field observation. The result is a publication that can sit alongside Bloomberg in the morning and Resident Advisor at midnight and feel at home in both contexts.

Research Philosophy

How We Work

We treat audience data as editorial material. Our research investigations combine primary data collection with qualitative fieldwork — which means our intelligence reports read like reported pieces, not white papers.

We conduct long-form field observations. We build original datasets. We interview people inside the systems we cover, not just adjacent to them. Our Signals briefs are tracked longitudinally, so readers can follow how a cultural shift develops across months and years.

Standards

Editorial Standards

Every analysis published in Cult Circulation is subject to editorial review. We distinguish clearly between observation, inference, and conclusion. When we make a claim about cultural direction, we show our reasoning. When our data has limitations, we say so.

We do not publish sponsored content. We do not accept advertising that conflicts with editorial independence. The Audience Intelligence section presents real research from real datasets. Methodology is available for all published reports.
